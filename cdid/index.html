Series ID search (beta)

This prototype has been developed to help users explore ONS economic data and search and download by series ID. The vast majority of economic data produced by ONS are given unique, randomly generated, four digit identifiers and user research has shown that many of our users use, and are familiar with, these identifiers.

If you are not already familiar with our data, or are looking for top level economic series we would recommend visiting the Key Economic Time Series page first.
