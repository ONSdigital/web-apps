Terminology and Acronyms



Series Name of the series
Series ID Unique four digit identifier
Units Unit of observation or measurement
SA/NSA SA: Seasonally Adjusted
NSA: Not Seasonally Adjusted
Seasonal adjustment is a statistical technique that attempts to measure and remove the influences of predictable seasonal patterns
CVM/CP CVM: Chained Volume Measures
CP: Current Prices
More information about Chained Volume Measures and Current Prices can be found on our GDP questions page
